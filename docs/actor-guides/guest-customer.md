# Guest Customer Guide

Primary surface: guest booking and secure links, future booking surfaces

## Purpose

Guest Customers should be able to make limited one-off bookings without creating a full permanent account first. This keeps the marketplace accessible while still requiring phone verification and safe transaction records.

## Current Capabilities

- Guest customer role and permission foundation exists.
- OTP and phone verification foundations exist.
- Full guest booking flow is planned later.

## Target Workflow

1. Browse available service categories and verified artisans.
2. Choose a service and provide job details.
3. Enter name, phone number, and service address.
4. Verify phone number with OTP.
5. Submit booking request.
6. Use secure links to track booking status.
7. Pay through the platform.
8. Optionally claim or upgrade to a registered customer account.

## Guest Rules

- Phone verification is required before submitting a booking.
- Guest access should be limited to the specific booking context.
- Guest links should not expose unrelated customer, artisan, payment, or address records.
- Reviews should be allowed only after completed paid work.

## Escalation Path

- Booking issue: use the secure booking link once implemented.
- Payment issue: keep the payment reference and phone number used for booking.
- Safety concern: contact platform support through the available support channel.

## Current Limitations

- Guest booking screens, secure status links, payment flow, and guest review flow are planned later.
- Guest account claiming is separate from agent-created artisan account claiming.

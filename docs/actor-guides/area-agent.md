# Area Agent Guide

Primary surface: `/agent`

## Purpose

Area Agents handle field execution. They support artisan onboarding, collect verification evidence, perform physical visits, and provide first-line local follow-up.

## Current Responsibilities

- View assigned territory queues.
- View artisan profiles in assigned territories or profiles they onboarded.
- Submit field visit records.
- Capture visit status, notes, coordinates, and checklist evidence.
- Support agent-assisted artisan onboarding.
- Flag incomplete, suspicious, or mismatched field evidence for LGA Admin review.

## Standard Workflow

1. Sign in with an Area Agent account.
2. Open `/agent`.
3. Review assigned KYC submissions and field visit tasks.
4. Visit the artisan's shop, market stall, workshop, estate, or service location.
5. Confirm physical presence, service type, and location consistency.
6. Record field visit status, notes, coordinates, and checklist evidence.
7. Submit findings for LGA Admin review.

## Field Visit Rules

- Use accurate territory and location information.
- Do not approve KYC directly; field evidence supports LGA or State review.
- Record failed visits honestly with notes.
- Flag identity mismatch, unsafe behavior, duplicate business records, or suspicious documents.
- Do not collect or store KYC evidence outside approved app workflows.

## Escalation Path

- Incomplete evidence: return to artisan or notify LGA Admin.
- Suspicious profile: flag to LGA Admin.
- Severe safety, fraud, or misconduct issue: escalate to LGA Admin immediately.
- Multi-territory or repeated issue: LGA Admin escalates to State Coordinator.

## Current Limitations

- A dedicated mobile field app is not implemented yet.
- Offline capture and later sync are planned later.
- Support case and dispute flows are planned later.
